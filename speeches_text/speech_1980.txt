<p data-HoCid="7432815" data-originallang="en">Madam Speaker, I am honoured to congratulate my friend and fellow Manitoban, Anita Neville, on her appointment as the 26th Lieutenant-Governor of Manitoba. Always a trailblazer, Anita is the third woman and first Jewish person to hold this distinguished position.</p>
<p data-HoCid="7432816" data-originallang="en">Ms. Neville has had a long and impressive background serving our community in Winnipeg. She was a trustee in the Winnipeg school division for over a decade, taking on leadership roles such as chair of the board.</p>
<p data-HoCid="7432817" data-originallang="en">She went on to become the member of Parliament for Winnipeg South Centre from 2000 to 2011 and served as the parliamentary secretary to the minister of Canadian heritage and the minister responsible for the status of women.</p>
<p data-HoCid="7432818" data-originallang="en">Throughout her time in public life, Anita was a strong advocate for promoting diversity and inclusion, reconciliation with indigenous peoples and stronger representation of women in politics.</p>
<p data-HoCid="7432819" data-originallang="en">I know the Hon. Anita Neville will carry out her duties with passion and dedication and be an outstanding representative of the Crown.</p>
