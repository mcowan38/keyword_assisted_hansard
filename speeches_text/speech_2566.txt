<p data-HoCid="7927911" data-originallang="fr">Mr. Speaker, the news that the House of Commons paid tribute to a Nazi Waffen-SS officer here, in the House of Commons, has sent shockwaves around the world.</p>
<p data-HoCid="7927912" data-originallang="fr">The incident embarrassed Canada, harmed victims of the Holocaust and gifted a propaganda tool to the Russians. The Prime Minister is solely responsible for guaranteeing the success of all visits by international leaders here in Canada.</p>
<p data-HoCid="7927913" data-originallang="fr">Will he personally take responsibility for this huge problem, which he helped cause?</p>
