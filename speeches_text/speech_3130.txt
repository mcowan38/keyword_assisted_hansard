<p data-HoCid="8059346" data-originallang="en">Mr. Speaker, we condemn Hamas, a terrorist organization. We condemn its actions against women and other civilians.</p>
<p data-HoCid="8059347" data-originallang="en">Today, we actually got good news from the region. The first hostages have been released under the agreement that was signed, and aid will imminently be flowing into the region.</p>
<p data-HoCid="8059348" data-originallang="en">We continue to call for the protection of Palestinian and Israeli civilians. We call for Canadian and foreign national hostages to be released, for foreign nationals to leave and for all the hostages to be released.</p>
