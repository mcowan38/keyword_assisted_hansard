<p data-HoCid="8277165" data-originallang="fr">Mr. Speaker, today in the House, we have had an important debate on an issue that is global in scope and very historic, but also heartbreaking. I know that all points of view expressed in the House come from the heart and from a fundamentally Canadian emotion that drives us to seek peace.</p>
<p data-HoCid="8277166" data-originallang="en">It is very much in this spirit that I move: </p>
<blockquote><p data-HoCid="8277167" data-originallang="en"> That the motion be amended as follows: </p>
<p data-HoCid="8277168" data-originallang="en"> (a) in paragraph (ii), by adding the word “being” after the word “victims”; </p>
<p data-HoCid="8277169" data-originallang="en"> (b) by replacing paragraph (iii) with the following: “Hamas is a listed terrorist organization in Canada whose attacks on October 7, 2023, killed nearly 1,200 Israelis and that over 100 hostages remain in Hamas captivity”; </p>
<p data-HoCid="8277170" data-originallang="en"> (c) in paragraph (iv), by replacing the words “millions of” with the words “1.7 million”; </p>
<p data-HoCid="8277171" data-originallang="en"> (d) by adding, after paragraph (vi), the following new paragraph: “all states, including Israel, have a right to defend themselves and in defending itself, Israel must respect international humanitarian law and the price of defeating Hamas cannot be the continuous suffering of all Palestinian civilians”; </p>
<p data-HoCid="8277172" data-originallang="en"> (e) by replacing paragraph (viii) with the following: “the increase in extremist settler violence against Palestinians and reports of Palestinian communities being forcibly removed from their lands in the West Bank”; </p>
<p data-HoCid="8277173" data-originallang="en"> (f) by replacing paragraph (a) with the following: “demand an immediate ceasefire, the release of all hostages, and Hamas must lay down its arms”; </p>
<p data-HoCid="8277174" data-originallang="en"> (g) by replacing paragraph (b) with the following: “cease the further authorization and transfer of arms exports to Israel to ensure compliance with Canada’s arms export regime and increase efforts to stop the illegal trade of arms, including to Hamas”; </p>
<p data-HoCid="8277175" data-originallang="en"> (h) by replacing paragraph (c) with the following: “ensure continued funding to the United Nations Relief and Works Agency (UNRWA) to meet the dire humanitarian need, engage with the United Nations internal investigation and independent review process, and ensure implementation of necessary long-term governance reforms and accountability measures”; </p>
<p data-HoCid="8277176" data-originallang="en"> (i) in paragraph (d) by deleting the words “, and support the work of the International Court of Justice and the International Criminal Court”; </p>
<p data-HoCid="8277177" data-originallang="en"> (j) by adding, after paragraph (d), the following new paragraph: “support the work of the International Court of Justice and the International Criminal Court”; </p>
<p data-HoCid="8277178" data-originallang="en"> (k) by replacing paragraph (f) with the following: “ensure Canadians trapped in Gaza can reach safety in Canada and expand access to the temporary resident visa program”; </p>
<p data-HoCid="8277179" data-originallang="en"> (l) by replacing paragraph (g) with the following: “sanction extremist settlers and maintain sanctions on Hamas leaders”; </p>
<p data-HoCid="8277180" data-originallang="en"> (m) by replacing paragraph (h) with the following: “reaffirm that settlements are illegal under international law and that settlements and settler violence are serious obstacles to a negotiated two-state solution, and advocate for an end to the decades long occupation of Palestinian territories”; and </p>
<p data-HoCid="8277181" data-originallang="en"> (n) by replacing paragraph (i) with the following: “work with international partners to actively pursue the goal of a comprehensive, just and lasting peace in the Middle East, including towards the establishment of the State of Palestine as part of a negotiated two-state solution, and maintain Canada’s position that Israel has a right to exist in peace and security with its neighbours”. </p>
</blockquote>
