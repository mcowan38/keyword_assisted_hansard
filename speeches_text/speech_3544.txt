<p data-HoCid="8245965" data-originallang="en">Mr. Speaker, more than eight million people in Sudan have been displace through 10 months of war. This has been devastating for people in Sudan and for their family members in Canada.</p>
<p data-HoCid="8245966" data-originallang="en">The special immigration measure for Ukrainians set important standards, and it should apply everywhere else. Canada showed that we can help those facing war and persecution when 220,000 Ukrainians got to safety. However, the Liberals put an arbitrary 1,000-person limit for people in Gaza and 3,250 for Sudan.</p>
<p data-HoCid="8245967" data-originallang="en">Will the minister lift these caps so that no Canadians with families in war zones are discriminated against?</p>
