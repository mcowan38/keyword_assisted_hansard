<p data-HoCid="7977975" data-originallang="en">Mr. Speaker, leaked emails from Global Affairs Canada reveal that the government has no plan for over 400 Canadians desperately trying to flee Gaza, as well as hundreds more in the West Bank.</p>
<p data-HoCid="7977976" data-originallang="en">In Gaza, they have no food, they have no water and the hospitals are crumbling. The minister and the <a data-HoCid="214296" href="/politicians/justin-trudeau/" title="Justin Trudeau">Prime Minister</a> have no answers for Canadians, and they refuse to call for an end to Israel's siege and for a full ceasefire.</p>
<p data-HoCid="7977977" data-originallang="en">What is the plan to evacuate Canadians out of Gaza and the West Bank? How many Canadians and Palestinians will die before the Liberals call for a ceasefire?</p>
