<p data-HoCid="7716697" data-originallang="en"> The U.S. legislation basically created an inter-agency working group that was charged with a duty to collect and organize Holocaust records from across the federal government and have the records be delivered to the National Archives. It then mandated the National Archives to set up a public archive of all these records.</p>
<p data-HoCid="7716698" data-originallang="en"> That was the gist of this federal legislation. It's quite progressive relative to what we have in Canada.</p>
