<p data-HoCid="8576881" data-originallang="en">Madam Speaker, I would like to quote article 51 of the United Nations charter, which states, “Nothing in the present Charter shall impair the inherent right of individual or collective self-defence if an armed attack occurs against a Member of the United Nations”.</p>
<p data-HoCid="8576882" data-originallang="en">I am wondering whether the member would comment on the state of Israel's right to defend itself under article 51 of the UN Charter.</p>
