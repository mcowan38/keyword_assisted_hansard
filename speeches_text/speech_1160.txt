<p data-HoCid="0" data-originallang="en">Mr. Speaker, Palestinian civilians are suffering profound hardship, as a result of the recent violence, displacement and loss of life and have endured unimaginable pain. All human beings are born with equal, unalienable rights. We must all work to uphold them.</p>
<p data-HoCid="0" data-originallang="en">Canadians, including residents of my community, are expressing deep concern over these rights, the urgent need for humanitarian assistance in Gaza and the West Bank, and unfettered access to provide this assistance. </p>
<p data-HoCid="0" data-originallang="en">Could the <a data-HoCid="253393" href="/politicians/karina-gould/" title="Karina Gould">Minister of International Development</a> tell the House how Canada will support Palestinians in coping with the effects of this devastating violence and work to build a foundation for lasting peace?</p>
