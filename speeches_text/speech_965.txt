<p data-HoCid="6672323" data-originallang="en">Mr. Speaker, as I have said numerous times, this government stands against anti-Semitism, and we have been very clear about that.</p>
<p data-HoCid="6672324" data-originallang="en">As soon as I learned about this material I was deeply concerned and contacted my officials, as well as UNRWA itself, to get to the bottom of this. I have been in touch with counterparts around the world, and we are working with UNRWA to ensure that the materials it teaches, which are provided by the jurisdiction in which it operates, meet UN values and uphold the principles of neutrality.</p>
