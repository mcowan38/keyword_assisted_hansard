<p data-HoCid="6483762" data-originallang="en">Madam Speaker, hatred and extremism often target diverse communities. We must stop anyone who seeks to harm others because of their race, religion or gender.</p>
<p data-HoCid="6483763" data-originallang="en"> Unfortunately, we continue to see hate manifested in our country. Recently, a Montreal synagogue was vandalized. In my home province of Quebec, we just marked the fourth anniversary of the Quebec City mosque attack. These events remind us of the painful impacts hate can have.</p>
<p data-HoCid="6483764" data-originallang="en">Can the <a data-HoCid="253386" href="/politicians/bill-blair/" title="Bill Blair">Minister of Public Safety</a> let the House know what new measures our government is taking to protect people from extremist violence and hate?</p>
