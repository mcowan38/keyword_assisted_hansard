<p data-HoCid="6236664" data-originallang="en">Madam Speaker, the Montreal Sikh community, inspired by the tenets of the religious tradition established by Guru Nanak, places the highest priority on the values of sharing and helping others. These values are lived out every Sunday in gurdwaras through langar community kitchens. They have also been clearly evident during the pandemic.</p>
<p data-HoCid="6236665" data-originallang="en">The Sikh community of Montreal has provided over 63,000 individually packaged snacks to health care workers at the Montreal General Hospital, the Centre hospitalier de l'Université de Montréal and the Jewish General Hospital and to staff in seniors homes. It has also provided 450 hot meals to staff in the Jewish General's ICU and emergency department.</p>
<p data-HoCid="6236666" data-originallang="en">The community has donated over $13,000 to both l'Hôpital du Sacré-Coeur-de-Montréal and the Lakeshore General Hospital for essential equipment.</p>
<p data-HoCid="6236667" data-originallang="en">We thank members of Montreal's Sikh community for their generosity and inspiring example.</p>
