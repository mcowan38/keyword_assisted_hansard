<p data-HoCid="8735367" data-originallang="en">Mr. Speaker, there have been discussions among the parties, and I believe if you seek it, you will find unanimous consent for the following motion. I move:</p>
<blockquote><p data-HoCid="8735368" data-originallang="en"> That the House: </p>
<p data-HoCid="8735369" data-originallang="en"> (a) firmly condemn the violent acts and the antisemitic gestures which took place during the demonstrations in Montreal on November 21 and 22; </p>
<p data-HoCid="8735370" data-originallang="en"> (b) condemn all attacks against the Jewish community and state clearly that Jewish Canadians, like all Canadians, have a right to live safely in this country; </p>
<p data-HoCid="8735371" data-originallang="en"> (c) condemn all forms of support for Hamas or any other terrorist group; and </p>
<p data-HoCid="8735372" data-originallang="en"> (d) reaffirm the right to peaceful and free protest. </p>
</blockquote>
