<p data-HoCid="7956595" data-originallang="en">Mr. Speaker, our government is very clear that we support the State of Israel and recognize Israel's right to defend itself within international law.</p>
<p data-HoCid="7956596" data-originallang="en">As the <a data-HoCid="214296" href="/politicians/justin-trudeau/" title="Justin Trudeau">Prime Minister</a> has said, we are deeply concerned by the dire humanitarian situation in Gaza. International law must be respected. Canada will continue to support civilians of Gaza with urgent humanitarian needs. That is why we announced an initial commitment of $10 million in humanitarian assistance to trusted partners.</p>
