<p data-HoCid="6689799" data-originallang="en">Mr. Speaker, in the middle of the largest global health crisis seen in over a century, seniors need more than a token benefits increase. What seniors really need are vaccinations so that they can enjoy the rest of their years secure from COVID-19.</p>
<p data-HoCid="6689800" data-originallang="en">Countries such as Israel, the United States, the United Kingdom and Singapore are beating Canada to vaccinate their people. When will the <a data-HoCid="253395" href="/politicians/patty-hajdu/" title="Patty Hajdu">Minister of Health</a> account for her failure to bring wide distribution of vaccines?</p>
