<p data-HoCid="6679694" data-originallang="en">Mr. Speaker, I would like to thank my colleague from <a data-HoCid="269650" href="/politicians/yaara-saks/" title="Ya'ara Saks">York Centre</a> for her hard work for her constituents and her advocacy.</p>
<p data-HoCid="6679695" data-originallang="en">This month we have the opportunity to celebrate and learn about Jewish communities in Canada, along with their history of courage and resilience. This is also a time to reaffirm our commitment to combat xenophobia and anti-Semitism.</p>
<p data-HoCid="6679696" data-originallang="en">Our government is committed to fighting hatred, prejudice and discrimination in all their forms through important measures such as Canada's anti-racism strategy 2019-22, in which the International Holocaust Remembrance Alliance's definition of anti-Semitism was formally adopted.</p>
