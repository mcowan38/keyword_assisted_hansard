<p data-HoCid="7200087" data-originallang="fr">Madam Speaker, I would like to remind members of a historical fact.</p>
<p data-HoCid="7200088" data-originallang="fr">The first Jewish person to be elected to public office in the entire British Empire was elected in Trois‑Rivières, Quebec. He did not have the right to sit in this chamber because of his religion.</p>
<p data-HoCid="7200089" data-originallang="fr">We will not take any lessons about diversity from Canada.</p>
