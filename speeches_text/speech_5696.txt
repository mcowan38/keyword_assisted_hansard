<p data-HoCid="8784320" data-originallang="en"> The recommendation we put forward stems from a joint letter signed by my organization, the Centre for Israel and Jewish Affairs, and the Friends of Simon Wiesenthal Center. It dealt solely with the emblems and the flags of listed terror entities. However, B'nai Brith would unequivocally support the construction of additional legislation that would prevent the display of Nazi iconography.</p>
<p data-HoCid="8784321" data-originallang="en">Just because the Nazi party is not a listed terror entity here in Canada, it does not mean that the flying of the Nazi swastika or the display of Nazi iconography should be permitted. That is, certainly, something that we believe would fall under the reasonable limitations of the freedom of expression, as enumerated in the charter.</p>
