<p data-HoCid="6791901" data-originallang="en">Canada's policy is long-standing. We do not recognize permanent Israeli control over the territories from the 1967 conflict, which includes east Jerusalem, Gaza, the Golan Heights and the West Bank. This is what makes the settlements the Israelis are building in those territories illegal and contrary to the Fourth Geneva Convention.</p>
<p data-HoCid="6791902" data-originallang="en">I very much agree that we need to work on the two-state solution, step by step, so that we can improve the lives of all in the region.</p>
