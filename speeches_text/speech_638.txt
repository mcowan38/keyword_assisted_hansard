<p data-HoCid="6503490" data-originallang="en"> “None is too many.”</p>
<p data-HoCid="6503491" data-originallang="fr">However, our country has also been on the right side of history. One has only to think of Mr. Mulroney, who showed real leadership fighting apartheid in South Africa. </p>
